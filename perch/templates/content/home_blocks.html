Explore. Relax. Enjoy.

The camps are organised by Waldorf teachers, public and private teachers together, some of whom are also Involved in the Camphill movement and who provide educational programs for the children.

We aim to provide our children not only English Language but also activities and life skills which includes creative drama, singing, cooking, dynamic movement, games, paintings and more in a beautiful national park in the UK.

It is a wonderful opportunity for children from all backgrounds for self-expression and creativity in a safe environment. Our teachers try to bring real life understandings to children with care and experiences.

Christian camps are also organised with the co-operation of church members in the UK.
